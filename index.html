Crystals Collector Game

Directions:

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals available. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

How to Win:

You win the game by matching your total score with the random number given to you.

NOTE:

  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crytal.
